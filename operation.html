Objective & Significance

Operation Sindoor was launched to protect India's borders and ensure the safety of people living in remote areas. The name "Sindoor" stands for sacrifice, strength, and love for the nation.

Strategic Areas

Targeted preemptive action in mountainous and remote regions with possible threat activities.

Legacy

Operation Sindoor stands as a proud chapter in India's defense narrative. It not only safeguarded critical regions but also sent a strong message of resilience to adversaries.

Message

India does not raise its voice in conflict, but its silence is backed by unmatched strength.
